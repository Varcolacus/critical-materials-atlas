Method · mechanism · falsification

Does the companion follow its host?

The price test claimed a by-product metal’s fate is set by its host’s cycle, not its own demand. That is a mechanism, and mechanisms can be checked. So: correlate each companion’s price against its host commodity (World Bank spot prices) — and against the general metals cycle as a control — and see which companions are genuinely gated by the metal they ride on.

How the coupling is measured (and the confound)

Host prices are World Bank Pink Sheet spot prices (aluminium, copper, nickel, zinc, lead, tin, iron ore, gold, platinum, coal, gas), annual 2002–2024. Companion prices are the atlas’s implied trade unit values. We correlate annual log-returns, companion vs host, and report the best-matching host. As a control we also correlate each companion against a base-metals cycle (mean of the six LME base metals) — a companion is host-specific only if it tracks its own host clearly more than the common cycle.

Confound (important): all commodities co-move on the macro cycle, so every correlation is inflated by a common factor — that is exactly why the base-cycle control matters. Trade unit values are noisy; ~22 annual points; hafnium/zirconium and a few hosts have no spot series. Directional evidence, not proof of causation. Inputs: volume.json × Pink Sheet → host_coupling.json.

Where the chain of reasoning has arrived

Five layers ago the atlas noticed satellites can’t name a mineral; that opened companionality, which opened the squeeze, which opened the price test, which posed this mechanism — and here it either holds or it doesn’t, in public data. Where a companion tracks its host tightly and beyond the common cycle, the “host decides” story is earned; where it doesn’t, the honest read is that trade unit values are too coarse to see it, or the metal has enough of its own market to break free. Either way the next child is set: swap noisy trade unit values for real reported prices where they exist (cobalt, lithium, the PGMs) and re-run the coupling on clean series. The atlas keeps interrogating its own last answer.
