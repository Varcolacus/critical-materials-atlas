Context

The refined-cobalt dependency (the metal alone is diversified): China refines ~62% of the EU's cobalt-oxide imports - the battery-cathode precursor. The naive view blames Belgium (~43%, Umicore's refining/clearing hub); the refined-product origin is China, while the MINE origin is DR Congo (~76% of world cobalt mining) - the trade ledger shows the refiner, not the mine.
