Two layers, shown together. ● Buy from = the true country of origin of the refined import (correcting the NL/BE "Rotterdam" port effect). ● Mined in = where the raw material is actually dug (USGS). Often they differ: the EU buys refined cobalt from China, but it's mined in the Congo — China is the refinery, not the mine. Each row shows both; ⚠ marks a mismatch.
