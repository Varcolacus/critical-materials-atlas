Naive rankings by importing member state show where goods are customs-cleared, not where they come from — distorted by the NL/BE port effect. Corrected rankings treat the EU as one entity and rank by true country of origin. The gap is the whole point: China is the single largest source but the true origin of under a third of these materials — the two most concentrated dependencies are the US (beryllium) and Turkey (boron).
