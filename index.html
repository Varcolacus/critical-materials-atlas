Two corrections in one tool. First: trade stats credit the EU port where goods clear (the NL/BE "Rotterdam effect"), so we rank by true country of origin instead. Second — and deeper: that origin is often just the refinery. The EU buys refined cobalt from China, but it's mined in the Congo. Flip the buy from / mined in switch to see China shrink and the real mines appear. Click any material for both layers.
