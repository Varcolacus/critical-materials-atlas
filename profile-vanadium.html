The origin gap. Ferro-vanadium is exported mainly by 🇦🇹 Austria (21% of world trade) but mined mainly in 🇨🇳 China (68%), while Austria mines almost none of it. Taken at face value, import-origin statistics point to Austria as the source; one layer upstream, the real dependence is on China. Gap: +21 points.
