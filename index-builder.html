Build your own supply-risk index

Every risk index hides a choice of weights. So make the choice yours: drag the eight sliders and watch all 32 critical materials re-rank instantly — with the correlation to the atlas’s published index updating live. Nothing leaves your browser; it’s all computed here on the public data.

How it works. Each material is scored on eight risk criteria, each normalised to 0–1 (higher = riskier): mine and refining concentration, trade concentration, China’s share, the origin gap, lack of recycling, hard-to-substitute, and producer governance risk. Your weights combine them into a 0–100 score. The published supply-risk index and the data-driven entropy-TOPSIS are just two particular weightings — the correlations below show how close your index lands to each.
